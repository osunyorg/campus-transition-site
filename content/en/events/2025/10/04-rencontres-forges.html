Saturday, October 4, 2025, from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., take part in the Forges Meetings on the Just Transition   Come meet the world of research and the actors and actresses of our territory of the South Seine-et-Marne within our eco-location. This day will be an opportunity to:
share our experience with two training projects that directly address social justice issues, which are inseparable from ecological justice issues,
bring us together as actors and actors of the territory,
participate in a common reflection on ecology and social justice in the South Seine-et-Marne.
PROGRAMME 9.30 a.m.  : Home around a hot drink 10am  : Introduction and visit to the eco-location 11:00 Round table: " How can we make a just transition with all of us in our territories? "with Noémie Paté (ICP), Elisabeth Dubujet (IDF Region), Solène Tournus (ETRE Network) and Maÿlis Dupont (FAS) 12:30 : Shared meal 2pm: Refunds and trade around our two projects Transition Just 17h : Workshops of collective intelligence " Think together the transition just by and for the territory " Come many, many!

Saturday, October 4, 2025, from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., take part in the Forges Meetings on the Just Transition 

Come meet the world of research and the actors and actresses of our territory of the South Seine-et-Marne within our eco-location.

This day will be an opportunity to:

  • share our experience with two training projects that directly address social justice issues, which are inseparable from ecological justice issues,
  • bring us together as actors and actors of the territory,
  • participate in a common reflection on ecology and social justice in the South Seine-et-Marne.

PROGRAMME

9.30 a.m. : Home around a hot drink
10am : Introduction and visit to the eco-location
11:00 Round table: "How can we make a just transition with all of us in our territories?"with Noémie Paté (ICP), Elisabeth Dubujet (IDF Region), Solène Tournus (ETRE Network) and Maÿlis Dupont (FAS)

12:30 : Shared meal

2pm: Refunds and trade around our two projects Transition Just
17h : Workshops of collective intelligence "Think together the transition just by and for the territory"

Come many, many!
