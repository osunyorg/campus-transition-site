The Campus de la Transition opens these doors, come and meet us!

body: >-


One Saturday a month, from 10.30am to 5pm, the members of the association welcome you to present the place, its history and its ambitions!
To the programme:
- 10:30-10:45 : reception;
- from 10:45 to 12:45 visit of the site;
- from 12:45 pm to 2:00 pm: lunch from the bag (think about taking your picnic!);
- From 2 p.m. to 5 p.m.: a participatory project is sometimes proposed. Happy to meet you!
