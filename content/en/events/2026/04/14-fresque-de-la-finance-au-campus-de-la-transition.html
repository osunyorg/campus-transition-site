The Fresque de la Finance allows us to understand the functioning of finance and its connections with socio-ecological issues. Participants build the financial system and monetary flows and become aware of the impact of the financial world with practical cases and action levers. Money, nerve of war... What if he became the nerve of peace, joy, solidarity, a life in harmony? Often, too often, with the savings money of the French, banks finance projects of climate bombs, weapons, AI... With the "Our Count" tour, you will know how putting the power of money at the service of what you care about This tour, launched by Operation Milliard, is intended for anyone who wants to understand the financial system, how to align their savings with their values and how to then give them even more power! To change life, take care of what really matters, and change the world.

This workshop will take place from 2 to 5 p.m. on Tuesday, 14 April. To register, click below:
