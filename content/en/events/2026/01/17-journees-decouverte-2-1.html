On Saturday 17 January 2026, the Campus de la Transition opens its doors!

body: >-


One Saturday a month, from 10.30am to 4.30pm, the members of the association welcome you to present the place, its history and its ambitions!
To the programme:
- 10:30-10:45: reception;
- from 10:45 to 12:45: visit of the site;
- from 12:45 pm to 2:00 pm: lunch from the bag (think about taking your picnic!); - From 14h to 16h30 : Sobriety workshop, concept and feedback

Happy to meet you!
