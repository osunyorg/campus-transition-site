On Saturday 21 February 2026, from 9:45 am to 6 pm, the Campus de la Transition welcomes you to its eco-location in the South Seine-et-Marne for a meeting around food.

body: >-

This day will be the occasion: - to explore the concept of Food Social Security in the presence of specialists and operators of the agricultural system; - to gather together between actors and actors of the territory;- to participate in a common reflection on the stakes and desirability of a Social Security of Food in the South Seine-et-Marne

PROGRAMME 9:45 am : Welcome around a hot drink 10:15: Introduction 10:25: [Keynote] SSA, right to food and food democracy - Dialing and status of the movement. 10.50 a.m. [Round table] Sud Seine-et-Marne: what are the ecological issues of the territory? 11:40 a.m. [Round table] South

Seine-et-Marne: an overview of the territorial food system. 12:30 : Meals 2:00 p.m. [Looks crossed] What are the challenges in implementing an SSA? 15h50 : Workshops of choice - Workshop 1: Methods and materials for mobilizing local people: stakeholder engagement and popular education. -
Workshop 2: Supply, distribution and support
production: logistics in common and fair remuneration of work. - Workshop 3: Multi-stakeholder cooperation issues: next steps to facilitate dialogue and build confidence. - Workshop 4: Local currency and territorial financing. 5 p.m.: Workshop return 5.45 p.m. Conclusion 6pm : End

This day will be the occasion:
- to explore the concept of Food Social Security in the presence of specialists and operators of the agricultural system;
- to gather together between actors and actors of the territory;- to participate in a common reflection on the stakes and desirability of a Social Security of Food in the South Seine-et-Marne

PROGRAMME
9:45 am : Welcome around a hot drink

10:15: Introduction

10:25: [Keynote] SSA, right to food and food democracy - Dialing and status of the movement.

10.50 a.m. [Round table] Sud Seine-et-Marne: what are the ecological issues of the territory?

11:40 a.m. [Round table] South Seine-et-Marne: an overview of the territorial food system.

12:30 : Meals

2:00 p.m. [Looks crossed] What are the challenges in implementing an SSA?

15h50 : Workshops of choice
- Workshop 1: Methods and materials for mobilizing local people: stakeholder engagement and popular education.
- Workshop 2: Supply, distribution and support production: logistics in common and fair remuneration of work.
- Workshop 3: Multi-stakeholder cooperation issues: next steps to facilitate dialogue and build confidence.
- Workshop 4: Local currency and territorial financing.

5 p.m.: Workshop return

5.45 p.m. Conclusion

6pm : End
