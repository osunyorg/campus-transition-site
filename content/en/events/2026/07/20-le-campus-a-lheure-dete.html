It's time for off-campus experiences and breaks

body: >-


As the campus doors close from August 1 to 15, experiences of all kinds continue. So is the epic of the "scam".


In common language, the "scam" refers to a scam. Paul and Adrien named their adventure as a foot of nose to the holding-up made by the automobile, which colonized our imaginations – mobility, travel, organization of the city. Their adventure is human. On board their cyclospace, on the roads of Val-de-Marne, they tell another report about distances, time and encounters. Discover their above manifesto and let yourself be dreamed, even after the summer.
