The campaign A Campus under construction, An increased impact is over and what a success!
We are happy and deeply grateful to announce that our crowdfunding campaign "A Campus Under Construction, An Increased Impact" is now over Hey ! For several weeks, you have been many to accompany us, to believe in this vision of a more welcoming, inspiring and more committed Campus for the ecological and solidarity transition.
Thanks to each of you , we have completed magnificent collection stages on HelloAsso, our solidarity financing platform: a tremendous mobilization of the community which demonstrates commitment to this collective project. To donors, ambassadors and ambassadors of this project, we send a huge Thank you   Each contribution - whether it was modest or consistent - made a real difference. Your commitment gives us the necessary impetus to continue the renovation of the school, improve our capacity and offer an ever more beautiful and inclusive hospitality to all those who wish to learn, share and engage.
Your support is not limited to an amount: it means trust, enthusiasm and common will to transform our spaces into places of life, thought and action . Thanks to your mobilization, work has already begun for the renovation of spaces receiving from the public, the development and improvement of training rooms and the renovation of rooms. Again: Thank you ! Together, we continue to build a place where the future is co-built in conviviality and boldness.

We are happy and deeply grateful to announce that our crowdfunding campaign "A Campus Under Construction, An Increased Impact" is now over

Hey !
For several weeks, you have been many to accompany us, to believe in this vision of a more welcoming, inspiring and more committed Campus for the ecological and solidarity transition. Thanks to each of you, we have completed magnificent collection stages on HelloAsso, our solidarity financing platform:a tremendous mobilization of the community which demonstrates commitment to this collective project.

To donors, ambassadors and ambassadors of this project, we send a huge Thank you 

Each contribution - whether it was modest or consistent - made a real difference. Your commitment gives us the necessary impetus to continue the renovation of the school, improve our capacity and offer an ever more beautiful and inclusive hospitality to all those who wish to learn, share and engage. Your support is not limited to an amount: it means trust, enthusiasm and common will to transform our spaces into places of life, thought and action.

Thanks to your mobilization, work has already begun for the renovation of spaces receiving from the public, the development and improvement of training rooms and the renovation of rooms.

Again: Thank you !

Together, we continue to build a place where the future is co-built in conviviality and boldness.
