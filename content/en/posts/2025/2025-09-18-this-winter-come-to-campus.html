body: >-


Are you a researcher, writer, artist, thinker, doctoral student or just curious to experience a sober lifestyle? In December and January, come create and take time for you and your projects by reconnecting to the essentials in a calm and stimulating setting. A regenerative parenthesis At Campus, enjoy a sobriety experience that is conducive to rest, connection and exchange. A unique experience Take part in the life of the eco-location, and explore new ways for a more sustainable and just world. Multipurpose workspaces You will have access to many versatile and adaptable workspaces, inside or in the heart of the garden. Rates
Basic rate: 95 € per day per person
Reduced rate on individual registration: 65 € per day per person
Solidarity rate: 50 € per day per person
All inclusive (accommodation, vegetarian meals, access to work spaces, optional participation in workshops and discussions facilitated by the Campus...)
Note that during your residency you will have access to more than 4,000 books in 186 categories: "Philosophie du Soin", "Macro-Economy", "History", "Organization Theory"... Most of the books come from donations from active members who participated in the foundation of the association, as well as from the personal library of our President Cécile Renouard.
