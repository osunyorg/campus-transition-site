Executive summary
This "campus transformation" concerns both the thermal renovation of buildings and the evolution of educational models. The campus is undoubtedly the place where the future is built and where today's social and ecological transformation of our societies is being played out and accelerated. With Pedagogy (tp), this book is part of the collection of Petits Manuels de la Transition published in the wake of the "Manuel de la Grande Transition" and which are committed to deepening the key issues of ecological, economic, social and cultural transitions.

The collection of "Small Manuals of the Great Transition"
These manuals aim to deepen the stakes of the Great Transition in the teachings. Piloted by specialists in each field, these thirteen volumes cover the core themes and disciplines.
