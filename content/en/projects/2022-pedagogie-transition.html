About
According to a recent study 85% of students are worried about the future with regard to climate change. By discovering the consequences of biodiversity loss or the effects of climate change, anxiety, incomprehension or anger point out among young people, increased by the lack of a political-economic response. How can we reopen a desirable horizon? Undoubtedly by the commitment of the entire educational community, called to make the teachings re-enter: to reconnect them with the reality of the planetary limits and the fragility of the Living. Our mission, teachers or professionals in education, is to revive the courage, hope and wonder of the current education system. Beyond the disciplines, it is also mutual aid, responsibility, questioning, discernment and the right to error that we must teach in order to get out of the current impasses. Good news: inspiring initiatives are multiplying around the world. This modest but indispensable small manual offers you some concrete ways to transform the educational offer and teaching practices within your institutions and courses!
