Coming from the Paris region
First step: join Montereau From Lyon station, choose :
the transilian R
the TER Burgundy direction Laroche-Migennes
In particular:
Train frequency : every hour
Duration: 1 hour from Lyon station
Cost : Montereau being in Seine-et-Marne in the Île-de-France region, the Navigo pass works. Otherwise, the ticket costs €2.5.
These trains also pass through Melun and Fontainebleau.

L e bus 3307 drop you directly at the Campus (jump of Forges).
Buses 3301 (Freedom) and 3304 or 3308 (Gardeloup stop) can also be an option.

Coming from elsewhere to France
There are two possibilities :
First join the Paris region and then come to the Campus by public transport.



If you have no other solutions than to come by car, opt for carpooling

The Campus is located near exit 17 "Forges" of the A5 motorway, which has a stopping area. Only 2 km (20 minutes walk) are required to reach the Campus.

To know  : in addition to controlling its carbon borrowing,

walking allows you to stay fit and preserve your health.
World Health Organization (WHO)
recommends taking 10,000 steps per day
