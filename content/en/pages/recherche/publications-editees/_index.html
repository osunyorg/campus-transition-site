Experiences and Testimonies

Action research at the Campus has its starting point in the projects and experiments conducted in Forges, which it addresses as examples of issues relevant to society at large. The series "Experiences et Testimonies du Campus de la Transition" aims to contribute to the Francophone scientific discourse through a series of "non-conventional" works, where interdisciplinary and transdisciplinary reflection is based on field experience and the very concrete problems it poses for researchers and practitioners.
