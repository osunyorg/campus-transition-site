Experiences and testimonies

Action research at Campus has its starting point in projects and experiments conducted in Forges , which it addresses as examples of issues relevant to society in the broad sense. The series "Experiences and Testimonials of the Campus de la Transition" seeks to contribute to the Francophone scientific discourse through a series of "non-conventional" works, where interdisciplinary and transdisciplinary reflection is based on experience in the field and on very concrete problems for researchers and practitioners.


"The "board researcher": Research at the Transition Campus as a near future", by Luigi Russia

"A walking bike workshop: speculative design for soft mobility", by Émilie Cros, Florent Haffner and François de la Noue (with Luigi Russia)

"Allowing time: the challenges of training and training at the Transition Campus", by Luigi Russi (with Baptiste Cuche et al.)

"Less heat without heating: an energy sobriety experience", by Florence Drouet (with Florent Haffner and Luigi Russi)
