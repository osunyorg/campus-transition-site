About
Our offer of immersive seminars is available specifically for those involved in the search: university laboratories and research centres. These seminars are spaces for collective reflection where researchers, students and local actors meet to co-build innovative solutions to environmental and social issues.


Castle and Park - Campus de la Transition

Our Goals

inspiring
Taking time for reflection and
the preparation of joint documents
Live an immersive experience
original to nourish commitment
individual and collective

The Experience
What will you live for ?
These seminars offer a unique immersive experience in an environment conducive to action-research and field experimentation. You will be immersed in concrete projects of ecological and social transition, in collaboration with members of the FORTES collective (Facilitators of Transition for Social Experimentation) and partners of the community, business and citizen world. You will have the opportunity to: - Explore transition territories - Starting from practice, study local transition dynamics, resilience strategies and social innovations. - Collaborate with field actors - Enter into dialogue with committed civil society actors, communities and businesses to confront your research with the realities of the field. - Co-create durable solutions - Conduct concrete action research projects in collaboration with local communities to transform knowledge into real action.

Why choose these immersive seminars for your research teams ?
- Strengthening action research - An immersive approach where research results are directly applied in transition projects. You work on real and tangible issues, and your research feeds into operational solutions. - Stimulate interdisciplinarity - Come and enrich your work with experts from a variety of backgrounds: scientists, citizens, private sector professionals, etc. Interaction with different profiles is at the heart of the Transition Campus approach. - Valuing collective commitment - The Campus is a veritable laboratory of social experimentation where the collective dimension is paramount. You will be involved in action-research projects that promote the social link, inclusion and transformation of practices at the local level.


Working and user-friendly spaces

A unique framework
Our research seminars are held at Campus, an eco-location designed for experimentation. As such, the Campus is at the heart of action research projects in collaboration with local communities, businesses, administrations and citizens. You will be able to draw on these projects, analyse them and actively contribute to them.

Where ?
At the Campus de la Transition, 1h from Paris, an eco-place and place of experimentation of the transition, conducive to collective reflection and exchange. Far from classical seminar or training venues, you will be immersed in a stimulating environment, designed to encourage inspiration and action.


The garden - place of learning and contemplation


Our accommodation, catering and rest areas

Who is this experiment for ?
These seminars are open to university laboratories, researchers, doctoral students and research teams involved in social and ecological innovation projects. They are particularly suitable for teams interested in participatory methods, applied research and studying complex systems. Whether you're in the humanities, social, ecological or engineering sciences, these seminars are an excellent way to advance your work in an experimental and collaborative context.

Terms and Conditions

In immersion at the Campus, our offer of research seminars combines team time, workshops and interventions according to your requests, and time of conviviality and contribution to the life of the eco-location.

Contact

Vous souhaitez plus d'informations ou discuter de votre projet ? Nous sommes à votre écoute pour échanger.
