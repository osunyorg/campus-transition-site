Are you a researcher, writer, artist, thinker, doctoral student or just curious to experience a sober lifestyle ? Come to the Campus in residence !

body: >-

About
Coming to the Campus is taking time for you and your projects by reconnecting to the essentials in a quiet and stimulating setting. A regenerative parenthesis At Campus, enjoy a sobriety experience that is conducive to rest, connection and exchange. A unique experience Take part in the life of the eco-location, and explore new ways for a more sustainable and just world. Multipurpose workspaces You will have access to many versatile and adaptable workspaces, inside or in the heart of the garden. Rates
Basic rate : 95 € per day per person
Reduced rate on individual registration: 65 € per day per person
Solidarity rate : 50 € per day per person
All inclusive (accommodation, vegetarian meals, access to work spaces, optional participation in workshops and discussions facilitated by the Campus...)
Note that during your residency you will have access to more than 4,000 books in 186 categories: "Philosophie du Soin", "Macro-Economy", "History", "Organization Theory"... Most of the books come from donations from active members who participated in the foundation of the association, as well as from the personal library of our President Cécile Renouard.


Castle and Park - Transition Campus

A unique framework
The research residences take place at Campus, an eco-location designed for experimentation. As such, the Campus is at the heart of action research projects in collaboration with local communities, businesses, administrations and citizens. You will be able to draw on these projects, analyse them and actively contribute to them.


Working and user-friendly spaces

Where ?
At the Campus de la Transition, 1h from Paris, an eco-place and place of experimentation of the transition, conducive to collective reflection and exchange. Far from classical seminar or training venues, you will be immersed in a stimulating environment, designed to encourage inspiration and action.


Our accommodation, catering and rest areas

Contact

Vous souhaitez plus d'informations ou discuter de votre projet ? Nous sommes à votre écoute pour échanger.
