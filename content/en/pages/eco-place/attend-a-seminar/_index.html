Everything about immersive seminars at a glance

Your Immersive Seminar

About
Our seminar offer offers an immersion in a committed and inspiring eco-location. According to your request, we tailor-made a unique experience, from inspiring seminar to real learning expedition on ecological and social transition issues.


Castle and Park - Campus de la Transition

Goals

Strengthening your team in a place
inspiring
Taking time for reflection and
the preparation of joint documents
Live an immersive experience
original to nurture individual and collective commitment

The expérience
What will you live for ?
A one- to several-day immersion in an inspiring eco-location, designed to allow you to discover, understand and experience the crucial issues of the ecological transition. We will propose you key themes: climate change, circular economy, responsible governance, new lifestyles, and more. You will be accompanied by experts, practitioners and pioneers of change.


The garden - place of learning and contemplation

How's it going ?
Each seminar is a real collective experience where you will learn while living the challenges of the transition from the interior. You will be able to test models of participatory governance, participate in creative workshops to imagine sustainable alternatives, and introduce you to resilience practices through real-world situations. You will leave with practical tools and an informed vision to become a driver of change.


Working and user-friendly spaces

Why participate in this immersive experience ?
- Discover - Experience inspiring meetings with transition actors (entrepreneurs, activists, researchers, etc.) who share their expertise and solutions. - Understand. Explore the underlying issues of the climate and social crisis through practical workshops, group discussions and real case studies. - Take action - Take action by experimenting with concrete solutions and transition tools that you can replicate in your own professional and personal life. - Reconnect - Enjoy a total immersion in a natural and soothing setting, fostering reflection, well-being and collective inspiration.

Where ?
At the Campus de la Transition, 1h from Paris, an eco-place and place of experimentation of the transition, conducive to collective reflection and exchange. Far from classical seminar or training venues, you will be immersed in a stimulating environment, designed to encourage inspiration and action.

Who is this experiment for ?
Any organization wishing to engage its teams on the issues of transition.


Our accommodation, catering and rest areas

Terms and Conditions

Our tailor-made seminar offer combines team time, workshops and interventions according to your requests, and time for conviviality and contribution to the life of the eco-location.

Contact

Vous souhaitez plus d'informations ou discuter de votre projet ? Nous sommes à votre écoute pour échanger.
