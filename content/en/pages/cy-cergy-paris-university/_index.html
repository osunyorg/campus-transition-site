The Campus de la Transition and CY Cergy Paris University have been committed since 2020 to a long-term partnership (8 years) aimed at transforming the university and its training offer in the light of ecological and social issues.

body: >-

About
Transition Campus directly accompanied CYU's central governance and management teams, as well as its teachers, teaching teams and staff to integrate systemic and radical consideration of ecological and social transition issues within the university and its training. This partnership, and the objectives it pursues, thus join the reflections and recommendations of the Jouzel-Abbadie report, submitted to the Minister of Higher Education and Research in February 2022. The establishment of a training transformation approach coherent and structured, driven by institutional governance; and the training of all staff It has identified environmental and social issues as crucial measures to bring about the paradigm shift needed to address these issues in higher education. This is also in line with the latest announcements and actions taken by MESR, in agreement with stakeholders (CGE, CPU in particular).

Transition Campus missions


Accompany the Presidency and management teams leading the transformation of the establishment and its training offer in relation to ecological and social transition issues;

Train and accompany teachers on the themes and pedagogies related to the ecological and social transition;

Training all staff and directorates the challenges of the ecological and social transition in order to create a common culture and enable the institution to be exemplary.
