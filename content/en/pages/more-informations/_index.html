A systemic vision of the "Great Transition" issues
In the ecological and social context, speaking of transition involves seeking to move from a contemporary situation marked by unsustainable trajectories to a state of societies characterized by sustainability and equity, vis-à-vis present and future generations. Behind all these objectives before us, many questions arise: how can we ensure these passages from realities marked by global warming, the destruction of life, poverty, inequalities, conflicts for resource appropriation, uncertainties? The "Great Transition" is therefore systemic : at the same time ecological, social, economic, cultural, political, citizen..., it also means "just".
