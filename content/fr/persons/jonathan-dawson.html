Jonathan Dawson is a fellow of Schumacher College, where he created and for 11 years led the innovative Regenerative Economics postgrad programme https://campus.dartington.org/regenerative-economics/. (lien externe)

He is the principal author of the Gaia Education sustainable economy curriculum http://www.gaiaeducation.org/, drawn from best practice within ecovillages worldwide. A former president of the Global Ecovillage Network, he is widely published on the subject of ecovillages and community economic development in the global South, where he worked as a consultant and project manager for 10 years.
