Nathalie Frascaria-Lacoste is Professor of Evolutionary Ecology at AgroParisTech. She is Director of the Ecology, Systematics and Evolution Laboratory (ESE, Université Paris-Saclay).

She is co-author of six book chapters, co-editor of the book 'Restaurer la nature pour atténuer les impacts du développement' published by Quae (May 2015) and 80 publications in international peer-reviewed journals. She led the Ecology group for the book "Manuel de la Grande Transition, Former pour Transformer" (Collectif FORTES, Editions Les Liens qui Libèrent, 2020).
